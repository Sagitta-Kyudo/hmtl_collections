AI-powered QA process Blueprint v1

A $0-Budget Initiative to Automate and Revolutionize Our QA

Our Mission & Core Challenges

Our goal is to create a highly automated QA workflow that moves from user story to executed test, even without a deep coding background.

The Challenges We Are Solving:

  • The "Manual Work" Bottleneck: Eliminating time-consuming manual test execution.
  • The "No-Coder" Hurdle: Empowering a non-developer to create and manage robust automation.
  • The "No-History" Problem: Gaining analytics and run history from our local-only Cypress setup.
  • The "$0 Budget" Constraint: Achieving all of the above with a stack of powerful, free tools.

4-Step AI-Powered Workflow

1
Scenario Generation

We feed the user story into Google Gemini. It analyzes the requirements and generates a comprehensive list of test scenarios (positive, negative, edge cases) in the structured Gherkin format.

2
Initial Script Creation (The "Recorder")

We open the built-in Cypress Recorder and simply click through the application as a user would. Cypress watches our actions and generates the raw, functional test script automatically.

3
Script Enhancement (The "Refiner")

We paste the raw script into a custom "Cypress Refiner" we build in LangFlow. This AI flow automatically adds crucial assertions, improves selectors, and adds comments, turning a brittle script into a robust test.

4
Execution & Reporting

The refined script is run locally. All results, test videos, and error screenshots are automatically sent to our free, self-hosted Currents.dev dashboard for full history and analysis.

Our $0-Budget Tool Stack

Google Gemini

For AI-powered test scenario and idea generation.

Cypress

For industry-standard test recording and local execution.

LangFlow

To build our custom "Script Refiner" AI workflow.

Currents.dev

Our free, self-hosted dashboard for all test history and reports.

Key Benefits & Next Steps

  • Solves All Key Challenges: This plan directly addresses our manual work, "no-code" gap, lack of history, and zero-budget constraints.
  • Empowering: It allows a single person to manage a sophisticated automation suite that would normally require a dedicated team.
  • Scalable: This blueprint creates a foundation we can easily build on, adding more complex tests and integrations in the future.
